About Monaware Labs

Discover our story, mission, and the people driving innovation

Our Story

Monaware Labs was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional business needs and modern technology solutions.

Starting as a small team of passionate developers and designers, we've grown into a full-service digital innovation agency. Our journey has been marked by countless successful projects, satisfied clients, and a commitment to excellence.

Today, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in technology, whether it's through cutting-edge web applications, blockchain solutions, or AI-driven platforms.

Every project we undertake is guided by our core values: innovation, integrity, and impact. We don't just build products; we create experiences that empower businesses and improve lives.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped Monaware Labs

2019 - The Beginning

Founded by John Okonkwo and Jane Adeyemi, Monaware Labs started with a mission to revolutionize digital solutions in Africa.

2020 - First Major Success

Launched SchoolHub, our flagship school management system, serving 50+ institutions and transforming educational administration.

2021 - Expansion & Recognition

Grew team to 10+ members and received recognition as one of Nigeria's top tech startups. Expanded into blockchain solutions.

2022 - Going Global

Started serving international clients and launched DevHub, our developer community platform, now home to 5000+ active developers.

2023 - 50+ Projects Milestone

Celebrated 50+ successful projects delivered, 30+ satisfied clients, and maintained a team of 15+ talented professionals.

2025 - The Future is Now

Continuing to innovate, launching AI-driven solutions and expanding our ecosystem to empower the next generation of digital entrepreneurs.
